Privacy Policy

Medi-Time is a medication reminder application.

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or medical data to external servers.

All reminder data is stored locally on the user's device using browser storage.

No data is shared with third parties.

Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.

This app is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.
